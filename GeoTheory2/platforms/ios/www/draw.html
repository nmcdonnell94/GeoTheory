Drawing Conics

Instructions

Position the blue points then move the green point to draw your conic.

What's a conic?

A conic is a curve that is formed by taking the intersection of a plane and a cone. There are three types:

  • parabola,
  • ellipse or circle,
  • hyperbola.

The tool above can help you draw them.

How is it done?

By choosing 5 points (blue points), these points will always define a conic and Pascal's theorem can help draw it by finding a 6th point that will always lie on the conic (the big red point).

Moving the green point will change where the big red point lies on the conic and hence how the conic is drawn.
